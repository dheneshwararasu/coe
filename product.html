INTRODUCTION
Based on a breakthrough technology called High-Dimensional Artificial Intelligence, CORE.ai supercharges your current technology and reimagines how shippers and carriers think about planning and operating their truckload networks.
Dynamic Load Planning and dispatching
CORE.ai changes how you think about managing your truckload network. Whether you are a shipper making sure you are moving the right loads with your dedicated fleet, or a carrier making sure the right drivers are taking the right load, CORE.ai plans for uncertainty, and constantly creates dynamic plans that have the highest probability of being profitable and on time, even if the unexpected happens.
Get ahead with Future VisibilityTM
A direct byproduct of High-Dimensional AI, Future VisibilityTM is one of the most significant paradigm shifts to the logistics industry. Whether that is knowing to secure purchased or spot capacity long before you ever knew you needed it, or understanding issues in your network more than a week into the future, CORE.ai is always two steps ahead.
